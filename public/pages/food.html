Arizona's Best Bites

Arizona boasts a rich and diverse food history that reflects its unique cultural heritage and geographical diversity. The state's culinary landscape is influenced by a blend of Native American, Mexican, Spanish, and cowboy traditions, as well as modern innovations. Below are a few of the must stop eateries here in Arizona.

Mexican

Guadalajara Grill

1220 E. Prince Rd, Tucson, AZ 85719 - Phone: 520-323-1022

With the average of 4.8 starts from over 5,500 reviews on google, Guadalajara Grill is considered to one of the best Mexican food locations in all of south Arizona. Located about 30 minutes north of the University of Arizona in Tucson, the restaurant specializes in tradition Mexican cuisine while giving an authentic feel of being south of the border.

The Mission

3815 N Brown Ave, Scottsdale, AZ 85251 - Phone: 480-656-5005

The Mission, located in Scottsdale Arizona, is an elegant twist to modern Latino cuisine. As they say on their website, “The sophisticated yet casual allure of The Mission seamlessly blends old world charm with modern elegance”. A restaurant for one of the more expensive nights, they offer a wide variety of Latino foods. Led by Head Chef, Matt Carter, The Mission’s menu will take you along a culinary journey across foods from Spain, Mexico, and Central/South America.

Italian

Caruso's

434 N 4th Ave, Tucson, AZ 85705 - Phone: 520-624-5765

Might be this editors most suggested restaurant on this list. Caruso’s, located in Tucson Arizona off the famous 4th avenue, is known for having one of the most authentic Italian home experience’s you can find anywhere in Arizona. Opening way back in 1938, Caruso’s has earned the honor of being the oldest continuously operated Italian restaurant in all of Arizona. Still located at their original location and now being operated by the 4th generation of owners, it is safe to say the Caruso’s has stayed close to their traditions. For those who love garlic bread, alfredo sauce, and wine this place is a must visit.

Fiamme Pizza

4706 E Sunrise Dr, Tucson, AZ 85718 - Phone: 520-529-5777

If you are looking for a bite of genuine Italian pizza, look no further than Fiamme Pizza, located in Northen Tucson. Ran by their head pizzaiolo, Scott Volpe, who not only has trained in Italy but also competes regularly in the World Pizza Games, is one of the many reasons Fiamme is the best place for authentic Neapolitan pizza.

Pizza Bianco

623 E Adams St, Phoenix, AZ 85004 - Phone: 602-258-8300

Pizza Bianco is one of the most well-known pizzerias in all of the world. It has the honor of being one of the favorite restaurant of the world-famous Chef, Gordan Ramsey. Started in 1988 the restaurant founder, Chris Bianco, since then has won multiple culinary awards. Including the highly regarded James Beard Award for Best Chef in 2003, and for Most Outstanding Restauranter in 2022. This goes to show the consistency in quality that Pizza Bianco has had over the past 20 years. Piazza Bianco was also featured on the first season of Netflix’s, Chef’s Table: Pizza.

Barbeque

Little Miss BBQ

4301 E University Dr, Phoenix, AZ 85034 - Phone: 602-437-1177

Regarded as one of the best BBQ spots in all of Arizona, Little Miss BBQ gives off a retro vibe while specializing in Texas-style BBQ. Located in the heart of Phoenix Arizona, with a small brick restaurant and smokers out back, Little Miss BBQ will make you feel like you traveled back in time.

Pork on a Fork BBQ & Catering

1515 W Deer Valley Rd, Phoenix, AZ 85027 - Phone: 623-434-1794

Pork on a Fork BBQ is a gem of a restaurant located in Phoenix Arizona. With catering being a big part of the company’s focus, the restaurant itself seems to be an afterthought almost, but that does not take away from the taste of the food. When first walking in, you order at a bar-like counter, then after getting your food walk down a hallway into a garage like building where they have seating for customers. This makes you feel as if you are back home and having a family barbeque. Because of the feel and great food, this is definitely a must visit for BBQ lovers.

Asian

Raijin Ramen

2955 E Speedway Blvd, Tucson, AZ 85716 - Phone: 520-795-3123

Located off a busy street in Tucson, Arizona, lies a gem of a ramen joint cherished by locals. This unassuming restaurant invites guests to enjoy and savor the flavors of an authentic Japanese cuisine. The menu boasts a tempting selection of traditional ramen bowls, each crafted with care and attention to detail. From the rich, pork-based tonkotsu to the savory shoyu and the umami-packed miso, there's a bowl to satisfy every palate. Next time you crave a soul-warming bowl of noodles, this hidden gem promises to satisfy.

Roka Akor

7299 N Scottsdale Rd, Scottsdale, AZ 85253 - Phone: 480-306-8800

Located within Scottsdale's esteemed Resort Corridor, just a stone's throw away from the vibrant Old Town, Roka Akor stands as a beacon of modern Japanese culinary excellence. Their menu is highlighted by a Wagyu steak sourced from renowned regions of Japan, as well as an enticing selection of grilled seafood and sushi. The Roka experience includes its signature robata grill which creates an experience different than any other steakhouses. Whether indulging in a romantic evening or gathering with friends, Roka Akor promises an unforgettable dining experience.

Fine Dining

Steak 44

5101 N 44th St, Phoenix, AZ 85018 - Phone: 602-271-4400

Amidst the bustling culinary scene of the greater Phoenix area, Steak 44 stands as a beacon of modern elegance and fine dining. Renowned for its impeccable service and sumptuous offerings, this esteemed steakhouse exudes an aura of sophistication and luxury. From melt-in-your-mouth filet mignon to creamy lobster tails, each dish is a testament to the restaurant's unwavering commitment to culinary excellence. Whether celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging for a night, Steak 44 promises to exceed even the loftiest of expectations, leaving you with memories of a truly extraordinary dining experience.

Charro Steak & Del Rey

188 E Broadway Blvd, Tucson, AZ 85701 - Phone: 520-485-1922

Located in the heart of the historic and lively downtown of Tucson, Arizona, Charro Steak & Del Rey delights diners with their exquisite Mexican high class style dining. With the culinary focus of sourcing only the highest quality ingredients, the restaurant takes pride in offering a menu that is both flavorful while having respect for the environment. To do this, Charro only uses grass-fed beef, sustainable seafood, and cage-free poultry for their dishes. Ensuring not only superior taste but also a conscious approach to dining. Originally rooted in Tucson, Charro also embraces local farming by trying to buy local, when possible, also the restaurant shares a special partnership with the University of Arizona’s Community School Garden program. Experience the true feeling of southwestern hospitality at Charro Steak & Del Rey. Also catch this restaurant be featured in the upcoming new show “Taste of the Border” on Discovery+.

Our Favorite's

Dillion’s Bayou

40202 N 87th Ave, Peoria, AZ 85383 - Phone: 928-501-2227

Dillion BBQ is a popular food chain here in Arizona, with 3 locations around the Phoenix valley, the one that stands out the most is their “Dillion’s Bayou” location, located in the west valley. What stands out about this location is that it sits on a lake, out in the west valley called Lake Pleasant. With the restaurant being on the lake, you can imagine the environment within the building. Often, having live bands performing, as well as the ability to jump in a lake at any given time, makes this restaurant a must visit.

Buck n Rider

7015 E Mayo Blvd, Phoenix, AZ 85054 - Phone: 480-561-5252

Buck n Rider is an elegant spin to the modern high class seafood experience. Having an oceanic theme to the restaurant, Buck n Rider will make you feel as if you stepped out of the hot Arizona desert and into a cool and breezy costal city. Having a continuously updated seafood and vegetable selections, it is safe to say, Buck n Rider gives its customers an amazing and genuine experience with every visit.
